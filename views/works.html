Steve Irwin spent his life promoting environmentalism with infectious enthusiasm. He educated millions of people around the world about animals and the environment while entertaining them with his programme, “Crocodile Hunter.” Irwin founded the Steve Irwin Conservation Foundation, which later changed its name to Wildlife Warriors Worldwide. He also started several other projects, including International Crocodile Rescue and the Lyn Irwin Memorial Fund, in memory of his mother, which benefits the Iron Bark Station Wildlife Rehabilitation Center.
